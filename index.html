    How to identify Deafness and Dumbness at an Early stage ?

    A child makes specific types of sound at specific phases of life. Making pre designated voices at specific intervals of age confirms that the child is not dumb.

    Children make following types of sound at certain phases of early age

    • Birth cry
    • Differential cry
    • Cooing sounds
    • Echo sounds
    • Babbling sounds
    • Echolalia speech
    • Jagron speech
    • True speech

    If everything goes smoothly as above, it means child is not deaf and dumb.

    Causes of Hearing impairments

    Certain diseases and accidents can lead to hearing impairment. However, before we start understanding the causes of the disease, let us try and understand the different types of hearing impairment.

    There are three basic types of impairments:

    • Hearing loss is the gradual loss of hearing, however, a person is able to decipher sounds when it is amplified or made louder. This is generally an age related hearing loss, however, there could be other causes for hearing loss.
    • Deafness is when a person is unable to understand speech, not even when someone is talking loudly to them. However, there is a slight chance that such persons can hear loud sounds and booms.
    • Profound deafness is the absolute inability to process sound in any form. This means that not only is the individual unable to make out speech, but cannot understand louder sounds like crashes and booms.
